Welcome to the Audio Annotator

The purpose of the specific web app is to randomly show recorded speeches to annotators, in order to choose the corresponding recording class.

How to use:

First you have to log in, if you have not already singed up, in order to know the annotator's profile.
Then, you have to hear a randomly selected audio and you have to provide the class that better describes the current audio.
The more audio you annotate, the better dataset is made.
